BIO

Career

IM A CLONE is a LoFi hip hop artist from Augusta, GA and currently resides in South Carolina. His melodic sample flipping and punchy bass lines create a very unique meditative and chill vibe. He grew up with a deep passion for R&B and old school hip hop growing up. He listened to and by on repeat in his child hood years. In high school he went to school in North Georgia and studied music. Ever since then his passion has turned into his emotional journal he can communicate through sounds. When he was 17, the dubstep artist went on Facebook and did a Q&A for his fans. IM A CLONE asked him what he used to “make his sounds” and his response was “I use Massive by Native Instruments, check em out.” He has owned a Maschine ever since and uses Massive to this day to make his bass lines to compliment his sleepy- chill music.
